Who Are We?

We are Jonathan, Markus, and Wayne, three students from Vancouver, BC, who have become alarmed with the growing problem of E-Waste in the world. After conducting research on the issue, we decided to create this code jam to help raise awareness towards the issue of E-Waste. All three of us like to code, and because COVID-19 is still prevalent, we decided that an online code jam would be more effective at accomplishing our goals.
